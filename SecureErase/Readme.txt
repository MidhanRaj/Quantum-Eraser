1. Problem Summary
Organizations disposing or recycling IT assets face a major risk of data leakage due to improper wiping of storage devices. Sensitive information—financial records, personal data, government files, and enterprise secrets—can be recovered if devices are not securely sanitized. There is a need for a reliable, automated, and standards-compliant data wiping solution to ensure complete data removal before recycling.
________________________________________
2. Objectives
•	Provide irreversible, secure data wiping for IT assets.
•	Support government and industry standards (NIST 800-88, DoD 5220.22-M, etc.).
•	Automate wiping, verification, logging, and certification.
•	Offer a trustworthy, auditable solution for recycling centers and enterprises.
________________________________________
3. Proposed Solution – EraseXpertz Secure Wipe Suite
A unified, secure software system that sanitizes storage devices and produces verified digital certificates.
Core Features
•	Advanced Wiping Algorithms: Single-pass, multi-pass, cryptographic erase, random-fill, zero-fill.
•	Device Coverage: HDD, SSD, NVMe, USB, enterprise storage.
•	Forensic Verification: Post-wipe hashing (SHA-256) + integrity checks.
•	Digital Wipe Certificate: Tamper-proof, timestamped, QR-coded proof of sanitization.
•	Central Dashboard: Monitor devices, logs, users, and compliance reports.
•	Bulk Automation: Batch wiping for large recycling facilities.
•	Security & Compliance: Role-based access, encrypted logs, secure storage.
